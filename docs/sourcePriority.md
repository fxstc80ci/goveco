# Source Priority

## Purpose

This document ranks sources for goveco data collection.

## Priority order

1. Audited public accounts and audited entity financial statements.
2. Official government inventories and registries.
3. Official budgets, estimates, plans, and results reports.
4. Official annual reports and corporate plans.
5. Official open datasets.
6. Auditor General and legislative officer reports.
7. Municipal audited statements and council reports.
8. Regulatory filings.
9. Procurement portals and contract disclosures.
10. Entity websites and press releases.
11. Credible secondary sources.
12. Estimates with disclosed methods.

## Federal anchor sources

- Statistics Canada Public Sector Universe.
- Treasury Board Inventory of Federal Organizations and Interests.
- Treasury Board Crown corporation list.
- Treasury Board Crown corporation holdings.
- Crown corporation financial datasets.
- GC InfoBase.
- Public Accounts of Canada.
- Departmental plans and departmental results reports.
- Main Estimates and Supplementary Estimates.
- Entity annual reports and corporate plans.

## Source registry rule

Every source must be recorded in `sources/sourceRegistry.csv` before it can be used in curated data.
